Providing successful commercial real estate investment opportunities since 1930, Rose Equity works on your behalf to source, evaluate and invest in the best real estate investments available anywhere. Finding the right opportunities at the right time is at the heart of our commitment to our clients.

From mixed-use properties and retail facilities to office complexes and more, our New York based private equity management team travels throughout the U.S. evaluating properties. Looking for unique opportunities, we skillfully vet each opportunity and practice disciplined underwriting to limit risk exposure and maximize the value of your capital investment.

Rose Equity can be your gateway to successful portfolio diversification. Visit with our principal today and see what investment opportunities are available for you.
